Soicism

Stoics is mainly about the search for wisdom and truth through the exercise of perfecting virtue and the dichotomy of Control. Stoic philosophers will constantly focus on the virtues and think of their day an their actions in the context of how well they lived out that virtue They do this though right action and a focus on what is in their control. Each day begins and ends with an examination of how virtues are display or not displayed their life and actions. Practice of these virtues will help us to tackle the challenges we face each day.
